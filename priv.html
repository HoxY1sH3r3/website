Privacy Policy for Legends RNG Discord Bot

Last Updated: [05-27-2025]

1. Data We Collect

When you use the Legends RNG bot, we may collect the following data:

  • User IDs: Used to uniquely identify users.
  • Command Usage Data: Information on how users interact with the bot (e.g., using /rng, /inventory, /gamble, /flex, /trade).
  • Inventory and Trade Data: Items or values associated with a user’s account.
  • Gambling Results: RNG and gamble command outcomes.
  • Trade Records: Data related to ongoing and completed trades.
  • Timestamps: Time and date of command usage.

2. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used to:

  • Provide core bot functionality (e.g., storing inventory, trade logs, RNG results).
  • Maintain fairness and prevent abuse (e.g., tracking gamble usage).
  • Enable persistent gameplay features by saving user data in a secure database.
  • Improve user experience and troubleshoot issues.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Your data is only accessible by the Legends RNG development team and is used solely for bot functionality.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is securely stored in a protected database.
  • Reasonable security measures are implemented to prevent unauthorized access or data breaches.
  • We retain user data for as long as the bot remains active or until a deletion request is made.

5. User Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data from our database.
  • Request access to the data we store about you.

To exercise these rights, contact the bot developers via Discord or through the support server.

6. Third-Party Services

The bot runs on the Discord platform and is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy. We do not control or take responsibility for Discord’s handling of your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here and take effect immediately.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to the Legends RNG development team via the support server or contact [@justacracker].
