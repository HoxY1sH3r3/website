Last Updated: [05-27-2025] Thank you for using Legends RNG, a Discord bot designed for entertainment purposes through RNG-based commands. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect data when you interact with our bot.

Some commands!

1. Data We Collect

When you use the Legends RNG bot, we may collect the following data: •User IDs: Used to uniquely identify users. •Command Usage Data: Information on how users interact with the bot (e.g., using /rng, /inventory, /gamble, /flex, /trade). •Inventory and Trade Data: Items or values associated with a user’s account. •Gambling Results: RNG and gamble command outcomes. •Trade Records: Data related to ongoing and completed trades. •Timestamps: Time and date of command usage.

2. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used to: •Provide core bot functionality (e.g., storing inventory, trade logs, RNG results). •Maintain fairness and prevent abuse (e.g., tracking gamble usage). •Enable persistent gameplay features by saving user data in a secure database. •Improve user experience and troubleshoot issues.
